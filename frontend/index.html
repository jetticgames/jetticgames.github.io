                            We collect only what we need to run Jettic and keep your account working.

                            • Account details you provide: username, email, password hash.

                            How we use it

                            • Operate and personalize the service (favorites, presence, recommendations).
                            • Secure the platform (fraud prevention, abuse detection, access controls).
                            • Improve performance and stability through diagnostics.
